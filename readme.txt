OhMyShop is a free hyperlocal e-commerce app. We are bringing a unique but old way of shopping from your their local shops. Here you can find your local shop which you know on this platform and you can just open their shop and see what products are they selling and place your order.

Main Features:
1)See all shops and their products on your phone.
2)Place order to them directly.
3)Delivery between 1-2 hours with no trust issue.
4)Directly call shopkeeper and can message them.
5)Can view products before going to shop.
6)Can manage your order cart very easily.
7)Can add multiple carts of the different shops.
8)Can inquire about the specific product.
9)Can find all types of shops like Footwear, Grocery, Stationary, Furniture, Clothing, Sports, Vegetables and Fruits and e.t.c.
